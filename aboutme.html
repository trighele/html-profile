ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Tom Righele, a Senior Application Developer at Marsh & McLennan, where I’ve been honing my skills and contributing to innovative projects for over four years. My journey in the tech world has seen me delve into app development, seamless app integration, and leading projects with a robust understanding of IT. Technology has always been a passion of mine, captivating me since childhood. While my expertise lies in backend development and integration, I also enjoy the creative process of building websites. Beyond software, I’m not afraid to explore the hardware side of things either—I’ve built multiple computers for myself, family, and friends, a hobby I find immensely rewarding. I’m 37 years old, married to my wonderful wife who is currently pregnant, and we share our home with our beloved pit bull, Georgie. In addition to my tech pursuits, I’m an avid sports fan with a deep love for the New York Yankees. Mariano Rivera is my all-time favorite player, and I pride myself on being a multi-time fantasy football champion, having won two competitive leagues last year. In my free time, I enjoy working out and spending quality time with family and friends. When it’s time to unwind, you might find me with a glass of tequila in hand. Known by a few nicknames—Bean, T Money, and Tommy Tequila—I embrace the fun and dynamic aspects of life.
